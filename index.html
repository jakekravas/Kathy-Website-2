Background & Credentials

My name is Kathy Kravas and I was born and raised in the Chicago suburbs. My lifelong passion for reading, writing and teaching has led me to form my own tutoring service. After nearly 15 years teaching junior high students, I’ve decided to start my own company where I can provide personalized literacy instruction to junior high and high school students. Unfortunately, the current health situation has caused many schools to significantly reduce, or completely eliminate, in-person academic instruction. Students learn best with focused, individualized and in-person teaching. Tailored Tutoring Service can fill any learning voids in a safe and supportive environment.

  • Certified secondary education teacher in English (grades 6-12)
  • Bachelors degree from University of Illinois, Champaign in English Education
  • 15 years experience teaching junior high English/Language Arts
  • 15 years experience teaching IEP, 504 and accelerated students, in addition to at-grade level students
  • 3 years teaching English/Language Arts to Dual Language Program students
  • Graduate coursework in junior high instruction as well as writing strategies
  • Outside school tutoring support for current and former students, including junior high, high school, and college level students
  • Raised children who attended D41 and D87
