About Me

My name is Kathy Kravas and I was born and raised in the Chicago suburbs. My lifelong passion for reading, writing and teaching has led me to form my own tutoring service. After nearly 15 years teaching junior high language arts in Schaumburg School District 54, I’ve decided the time is right to start my own company where I can provide personalized literacy instruction to junior high and high school students.

Unfortunately, our current health situation has caused most schools to drastically reduce in-person academic instruction. Students learn best with focused, individualized and in-person teaching. Background & Services

Background

  • Certified secondary education teacher in English (grades 6-12)
  • Bachelors degree from University of Illinois, Champaign in English Education grades 6-12
  • 15 years experience teaching junior high English/Language Arts
  • 15 years experience teaching IEP, 504 and accelerated students, in addition to at-grade level students
  • 3 years teaching English/Language Arts to Dual Language Program students
  • Graduate coursework in junior high instruction as well as writing strategies
  • Outside school tutoring support for current and former students, including junior high, high school, and college level students
  • Raised three children, two of whom attended D41 and D87

Areas of Expertise

  • Reading comprehension improvement
  • Reading stamina improvement
  • Literary analysis strategies
  • Essay writing (narrative, analytical/expository, argumentative)
  • Illinois Common Core State Standards mastery
  • Vocabulary and concept development
  • Grammar and usage
  • Test-taking strategies (including reducing anxiety/mental strategies)
  • ACT/SAT prep (English, Usage, Writing)

Services

  • In person, safe, individual or small group support/instruction
  • Support with school curriculum
  • Enhanced instruction outside of school curriculum
  • Accelerated literacy instruction for students needing extra challenge
  • Intervention instruction for those students needed extra support
